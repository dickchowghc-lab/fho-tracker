FHO+ Hourly Rate Time Tracker

Track your direct care, indirect care, and clinical administration hours for billing

Important Billing Rules

  • Hourly rate applies to enrolled patients only
  • Document total times for each category per day, not per patient
  • Billed in 15-minute units (8+ minute remainder rounds up)
  • Maximum 14 hours/day, 240 hours per 28 days
  • Maximum 25% of total hours for indirect + clinical admin combined
  • Maximum 5% of direct + indirect care for clinical admin

How to Use This Template

  1. Enter the Date for each time entry using the date picker
  2. Select the Category from the dropdown (Direct Care In Office, Direct Care Out of Office, Indirect Care, or Clinical Administration)
  3. Enter Start Time and End Time using the time picker (format adapts to your browser's locale settings)
  4. The spreadsheet will automatically calculate Duration, Rounded Units, and Hours
  5. Scroll down to see the Daily Summary showing total hours per category per day
  6. Click Export to CSV to save your data, or Import CSV to load previously saved data
